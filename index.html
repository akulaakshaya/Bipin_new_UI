Political Career

His leadership qualities were honed in the early student days of school to service the people.A man who is disciplained and always punctual. He is deeply involved in Indian Politics for more than four and a half decades. At present, he is the elected member of a Greater Visakhapatnam municipal corporation, 31 ward GVMC corporator on 17th November 2021 from YSR congress party. In 2019, He first contested independently for Visakhapatnam South Assembly Constituency as MLA and in 2020 contested independently for GVMC Corporator for 31 ward. Every time, choose Envelop symbol and campaigned door to door on bike as a single man in elections. For his honest and serving nature has been elected as a corporator from YSR congress party in by elections.

Profile

Mr. J Bipin Kumar Jain  is a visionary, entrepreneur, market analyst, social activist and politician, who with his dynamic leadership has empower individuals and small scale industries with his knowledge in business. He chose to enhance his service in the social and political arena motivated by Atal Bihari VajpayeeJi during his schooling. He shifted to Visakhapatnam from Mumbai in 2003 and started whole sale electronic business in north coastal of Andhra Pradesh. He loves challenges and ready to take risk by doing various new and Innovative businesses. His key strength is Known 9 languages so that can understand problems of people and solve their issues under jurisdiction. He loving to serve the people and feels himself it is his duty given by people.
